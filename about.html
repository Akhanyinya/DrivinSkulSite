Our Vision

To become the leading driving school in the region, recognized for excellence in instruction and commitment to producing safe drivers.

Our Objective

To provide exceptional services to our customers.

Core Values

  • Safety - We prioritize the safety of our students and instructors.
  • Professionalism - We maintain the highest standards of professionalism in all aspects of our services.
  • Integrity - We conduct our business with honesty and integrity.
  • Respect - We treat our students, staff, and partners with respect and courtesy.

About Us

HighView Driving School is a registered and NTSA accredited driving school that is main office located opp. KITI College along Nakuru-Nyahururu Road-Kenya. We are well equipped with trained and experienced Instructors who offers quality training to produce confident, responsible, and skilled drivers.

We have other branches in Njoro Town, Maili Kumi-Bahati and Subukia

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive and professional driving courses that ensures our students become confident, responsible, and skilled drivers.

Company Story

Founded in 2005, Highview Driving School started with a simple vision - to make roads safer by providing top-notch driving education. Over the years, we have successfully trained thousands of students, instilling in them the skills and knowledge necessary to become responsible and skilled drivers.

Our journey has been marked by dedication, hard work, and a passion for creating safer roads. As we continue to grow and expand our reach, we remain committed to delivering excellence in driving education and contributing positively to our community.
